# WiFi General
WiFi General administration is used to configure the WiFi capabilities of the ESP32. The following options are available for configuration of the ESP32 WiFi interface:  

- [Join WiFi](join-wifi)
- [Shutdown WiFi](shutdown-wifi)
- [Add SSID](add-ssid)
- [Generate SSIDS](generate-ssids)
- [Clear SSIDs](clear-ssids)
